School Arts and Culture

Was established in 1978

UWinnipeg offers a rich array of arts + cultural activity that makes UWinnipeg a dynamic place to learn. UWinnipeg proudly partners with other arts initiatives and organizations to contribute and enhance the creative and cultural vibrancy of our urban campus and our city. School Bookstore
Was built in 1980

The University of Winnipeg Bookstore is a full service campus store providing you with textbooks, course related materials, reference materials, and trade books. We are your one stop store for all your supplies, confectionary, magazines, greeting cards as well as University of Winnipeg clothing and souvenirs.
